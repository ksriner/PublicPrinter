FAQ's

Q: How do I find the print I want?

A: Use keywords in the search bar or browse through 3-D files under the Printable Objects tab.



Q: What do I do if I can’t find the print I want?

A: If the print you are looking for isn’t listed by one of our Creators, you can either post it on the Bounty Board
for Creators to contact you, or contact a Creator currently taking commissions through our messaging service.



Q: How do I know if my file is 3-D printable?

A: First ensure your file is an STL type file from a reputable source. Some files may not be properly prepared
for printing or have impossible dimensions unless they have been checked.



Q: What happens if I have a problem but I completed my transaction outside of Public Printer?

A: Public Printer only guarantees print quality and transaction safety when done through our website where we
can save all transaction history and agreed upon printing and details through our messaging service.



Q: How do I get started as a Creator with Public Printer?

A: Simply make an account, upload some STLs or pursue prints on the Bounty Board. It’s our mission to promote
and empower individual printers by giving them a platform to utilize their tools.



Q: What does Public Printer guarantee?

A: Public Printer guarantees the quality of any 3-D print ordered or your money back. Similarly for Creators we act
as a secure third party to ensure your payment is ready after your print is mailed.



Q: I have a problem with my print or my transaction. What should I do?

A: Contact support and fill out the fields above to send us a ticket and we will get back
to you as soon as possible to resolve your problem.
